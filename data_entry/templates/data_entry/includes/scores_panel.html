CHADS2 Score

CHADS2 assigns one point each for congestive heart failure (C), high blood pressure (H), age 75 or older (A), and diabetes (D), and two points for a previous stroke (S2) or transient ischemic attack, called a mini-stroke. The CHADS2 scoring system determines stroke risk.

CHA2DS2-VASc Score

The CHA2DS2-VASc score is a refinement of CHADS2 score and extends the latter by including additional common stroke risk factors such as age 65-74, female gender and vascular disease. In the CHA2DS2-VASc score, 'age 75 and above' also has extra weight, with 2 points.
